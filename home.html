Welcome to RTCSA 2025

IEEE RTCSA 2025, the 31th IEEE International Conference on Embedded and Real-Time Computing Systems and Application, will be held in Singapore on August 20-22, 2025. It will be co-located with NVMSA 2024. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore, subject to meeting IEEE Xplore's scope and quality requirements.
